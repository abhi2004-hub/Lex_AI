LEAVE AND LICENSE AGREEMENT
This Leave and License Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into on April 1, 2024, by and between:

PREMIER PROPERTIES PVT. LTD. ("Licensor")
AND
FRESH CAFE ENTERPRISES ("Licensee")

1. PREMISES
1.1. Licensor agrees to grant a license to Licensee to use the premises located at:
    Bandra Kurla Complex, Unit 200
    Mumbai, Maharashtra 400051
    (the "Premises")
1.2. The Premises consist of approximately 2,500 square feet of retail space.
1.3. Licensee shall have use of common areas including parking lot and customer restrooms.

2. TERM
2.1. The License term shall commence on May 1, 2024, and expire on April 30, 2029 (60 months term).
2.2. Licensee shall have the option to extend for one additional 36-month term with 90 days written notice.
2.3. Any extension shall be at the mutually agreed rental rate subject to a 15% escalation.

3. LICENSE FEE
3.1. Base License Fee shall be ₹4,50,000 per month, payable in advance on the first day of each month.
3.2. Fee shall be paid to Licensor at the address specified in Section 14 via NEFT/RTGS.
3.3. Late payments shall incur a penalty of 18% per annum if not received within 10 days of due date.
3.4. Interest-free Security Deposit of ₹27,00,000 (six months' fee) shall be paid upon execution of this Agreement.

4. USE OF PREMISES
4.1. Premises shall be used for operating a cafe and related food service business.
4.2. No other use shall be permitted without Licensor's prior written consent.
4.3. Licensee shall comply with all applicable local municipal laws and regulations, including BMC and FSSAI norms.

5. UTILITIES AND MAINTENANCE
5.1. Licensee shall be responsible for all utilities including electricity, water, and internet.
5.2. Licensor shall be responsible for structural repairs and building maintenance.
5.3. Licensee shall maintain the Premises in clean and good condition.

6. INSURANCE
6.1. Licensee shall maintain commercial general liability insurance with a minimum coverage of ₹5,00,00,000.
6.2. Licensee shall provide proof of insurance to Licensor within 10 days of execution.
6.3. Licensor shall maintain property insurance covering the building structure.

7. ALTERATIONS
7.1. Licensee shall not make civil alterations without Licensor's and BMS's prior written consent.
7.2. Any permanent alterations made by Licensee shall become property of Licensor upon termination.
7.3. Licensee shall restore Premises to original condition upon expiration, normal wear excepted.

8. ASSIGNMENT AND SUBLETTING
8.1. Licensee shall not assign this Agreement or sub-license the Premises without Licensor's written consent.
8.2. Licensor shall not unreasonably withhold consent to assignment or sub-licensing.
8.3. Licensee shall remain liable for all obligations under this Agreement.

9. DEFAULT
9.1. Default occurs if Licensee fails to pay the fee when due or breaches any material provision.
9.2. Upon default, Licensor may terminate the Agreement and regain possession as per the Maharashtra Rent Control Act, 1999 (where applicable).
9.3. Licensee shall be liable for all costs of re-letting, including reasonable attorney fees.

10. INDEMNIFICATION
10.1. Licensee shall indemnify Licensor against claims arising from Licensee's use of Premises.
10.2. Licensor shall indemnify Licensee against claims arising from building defects.
10.3. Both parties shall maintain appropriate insurance coverage.

11. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS
11.1. Licensee shall not store or use hazardous materials on the Premises.
11.2. All materials stored must comply with Central and State Pollution Control Board regulations.
11.3. Licensee shall be responsible for any environmental contamination caused by Licensee.

12. COMPLIANCE WITH LAWS
12.1. Licensee shall comply with all applicable building codes and health regulations.
12.2. Licensee shall obtain all necessary trade licenses and shop act registrations for operations.
12.3. Licensee shall be responsible for compliance with the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016.

13. ENTRY BY LICENSOR
13.1. Licensor may enter Premises at reasonable times with 24 hours notice.
13.2. Emergency entry may be made without notice in case of fire, flood, or other emergency.
13.3. Licensor shall show the Premises to prospective licensees during the last 90 days of the term.
