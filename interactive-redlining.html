Section 4. Limitation of Liability

4.1 Except for indemnity obligations under Section 9, in no event shall either party's total cumulative liability exceed the total amount paid by Customer to Provider in the twelve (12) month period immediately preceding the date of the claim.

4.2 Neither party shall be liable for any indirect, special, incidental, or consequential damages, including loss of profits, even if advised of the possibility of such damages.

Section 5. Data Privacy & AI Compliance

5.1 Provider represents and warrants that all processing of Personal Data will comply with applicable Privacy Laws, including the GDPR and CCPA.

5.2 Provider may use anonymized Customer Data to improve its AI models and general service offerings. Customer acknowledges that such usage does not constitute a breach of confidentiality.

High Risk: Section 4.1

The liability cap is unusually low for this industry standard.

...liability exceed the total amount paid by Customer to Provider in the twelve (12) month period...
...liability exceed the total amount paid by Customer to Provider in the twelve (12) month period three times (3x) the total Fees payable under this Agreement...

A 1x cap on historical fees provides insufficient protection against potential data breach costs. A 3x total contract value cap aligns with market standards for MSA enterprise deals.
