SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on February 15, 2024, between:

DIGITAL SOLUTIONS PVT. LTD. ("Service Provider")
AND
GLOBAL RETAIL INDIA LTD. ("Client")

1. SERVICES
1.1. Service Provider agrees to provide web development and maintenance services as detailed in Exhibit A.
1.2. Services include website design, database development, and ongoing technical support.
1.3. Service Provider shall perform services in a professional and workmanlike manner.

2. COMPENSATION
2.1. Client shall pay Service Provider a fixed fee of ₹75,00,000 for the initial project.
2.2. Monthly maintenance fee of ₹5,00,000 shall commence upon project completion.
2.3. Payment terms: Net 30 days from invoice date.
2.4. Late payments shall incur interest at 18% per annum as per the MSMED Act, 2006.

3. TERM AND TERMINATION
3.1. This Agreement shall commence on March 1, 2024, and continue for an initial term of 24 months.
3.2. Either party may terminate this Agreement with 60 days written notice.
3.3. Client may terminate for cause if Service Provider fails to perform obligations after 30 days written notice.
3.4. Service Provider may terminate for cause if Client fails to make payments when due.

4. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
4.1. Both parties agree to keep confidential all business information, technical data, and customer information.
4.2. Confidential information includes but is not limited to source code, business strategies, and financial data.
4.3. This confidentiality obligation shall survive termination for a period of 3 years.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS
5.1. All custom code and deliverables created by Service Provider shall become Client's property upon full payment.
5.2. Service Provider retains rights to pre-existing tools and methodologies.
5.3. Client grants Service Provider a license to use project outcomes for portfolio purposes.

6. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION
6.1. Service Provider's liability shall be limited to the total fees paid in the preceding 6 months.
6.2. Service Provider shall indemnify Client against claims arising from Service Provider's gross negligence.
6.3. Client shall indemnify Service Provider against claims arising from Client's breach of warranty.

7. WARRANTIES
7.1. Service Provider warrants that services will be performed with reasonable care and skill.
7.2. Service Provider warrants that deliverables will be free from material defects for 90 days.
7.3. Client warrants that all information provided is accurate and complete.

8. FORCE MAJEURE
8.1. Neither party shall be liable for delays or failures due to events beyond reasonable control.
8.2. Force majeure events include natural disasters, war, terrorism, and government actions.
8.3. Affected party must notify the other within 5 days of such events.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
9.1. Parties shall first attempt to resolve disputes through good faith negotiations.
9.2. If unresolved within 30 days, disputes shall be submitted to mediation.
9.3. If mediation fails, disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in New Delhi, India, under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

10. COMPLIANCE WITH LAWS
10.1. Both parties shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations of India.
10.2. Service Provider shall maintain all necessary licenses and certifications.
10.3. Services shall comply with data protection and privacy laws (e.g., Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023).
