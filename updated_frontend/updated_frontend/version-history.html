4.1 Force Majeure Events

ID: CLA-401-FM

Neither party shall be liable for any failure or delay in performance under this Agreement (other than for delay in the payment of money due and payable hereunder) to the extent said failures or delays are caused by acts of God, war, riot, or strike. proximately caused by Extraordinary Circumstances beyond the reasonable control of the performing party, specifically including global health emergencies and supply chain interruptions.

psychology AI RATIONALE

The previous wording was too narrow for modern enterprise risk. Updated to "Extraordinary Circumstances" to encompass pandemic-level events and global logistics failures which were previously unaddressed.
